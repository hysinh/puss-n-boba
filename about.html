The Cutest Kitties

Puss-n-Boba offers you an alternate fun place to work or a relaxing place to enjoy a boba in the company of our feline friends. Fast Wifi - Plenty of outlets - comfy seating

Meet | Work | Takeaway

Whether you are meeting friends or want a place to work or study for a bit, Puss-n-Boba is the place to be! With lots of seating and outlets, it's easy to work here. If you're just meeting a friend, we have plenty of comfy seating. And as always, grab a Boba tea and treat to go if you are on the move but need a bit of refreshment!

Who's that pussy cat in the window?

When you visit our cafe, you will enjoy our delicious boba and treats with the company of our resident kitties! They enjoy lounging around and can keep you company while you work or enjoy a chat with friends!

Our kitties live at the cafe. So, they always have access to a private kitty area to ensure that they have a quiet place to rest and eat and relax when they need it.

Sippin' Boba

We specialise in making a delicious boba and offer blended versions if you want a frappucino experience when the weather is warm. We also serve delicious Asian and Asian-inspired treats to go with our yummy boba tea. Our offerings change frequently - So, check us out for our latest offerings!

Easy to Access

Puss-n-Boba is conveniently located on the Church Road in the heart of Greystones. We are 3 minutes from the Greystones Train Station and a 5 minute walk to the seafront.
