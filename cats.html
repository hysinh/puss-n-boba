Our Cats

Salami

This tiny little lady is very friendly and enjoys sitting on people’s bags or on the table tops. She loves a nice pet and loves to watch the action from the front of the house

Teresa - Siamese

Teresa is our resident queen bee. She’s the oldest and definitely the most regal. She loves a cozy nap on the bar in the sunshine of the front window and a scratch between the ears.

Toby - Orange Tabby

Toby is our friendliest kitty and loves to be a lap cat. He’s usually up for cuddles and loves to snuggle in a warm lap on the couch.

Bruno

Bruno is a relaxed and curious cat and loves to sit next to our customers while they work on their laptops! He’s happy to watch your screen with you and keep you company.

Lady

Lady is our elegant, long-haired cat. Lady enjoys sitting in the front window with Teresa. Lady loves a gently pet between the ears and watching all the customers come and go.

Tako

Tako is very silly and loves to sit in boxes. If you check out a box from the counter and set it close to you, there’s a good chance, Tako will come sit in it!
